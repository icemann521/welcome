Hi There!
My name is Robert and I'm currently employed as a full-time Automated Broadcast Director at (Insert TV Station). I've been working in television news for 16 exciting years. I'm looking to take my creativity and passion for television news production and transition into a new career, a new career of Front End Development.

I first started my new adventure into HTML and CSS in the start of 2021. I haven't been working on this full time, but I've been slowly learning and expanding my skills. I've been using FreeCodeCamp, Youtube, & Udemy as my sources for learning and education. The Definitive Guide: Visual Presentation for the Web and HTML and CSS: Design and Build Websites, are the two books I research with to help aid with my learning of the principles of Front End Development.

For the past 16 years, I've worked on most aspects of television news production. I started my career right out of college in 2006 in Ft. Myers Florida as a production assistant and within 2 years, I worked my way up to graphics operation, technical directing and directing.

I worked for another 2 more years before moving to Houston TX as a full time Television Director. I've spent the past 12 years honing my craft and working in all sorts of stressful environments due to breaking news and breaking weather coverage.
